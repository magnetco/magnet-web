**API Development** — Custom integrations, data pipelines, and third-party connections. We make systems talk to each other.

**Content Management Systems** — Headless CMS architecture using Sanity, Contentful, and similar platforms. Content that editors love and developers don't fight.

**Ecommerce Platforms** — Online store development on Shopify, custom platforms, and headless commerce solutions.

**Infrastructure and DevOps** — Hosting, deployment, monitoring, and security. Sites that stay fast and stay up.

**Performance and Security** — Speed optimization, security hardening, and reliability engineering.

## Service Delivery Model

We work in three modes:

**Project** — Defined scope, timeline, and deliverables. Best for website builds, brand development, and platform launches.

**Retainer** — Ongoing partnership with dedicated hours. Best for marketing programs, continuous optimization, and long-term growth.

**Advisory** — Strategic guidance without execution. Best for teams that have capability but need direction.
